To be short, I am a Software Engineer. To be 2 words less short, I am a Full Stack Software Engineer. This portfolio site was created by me with zero framworks, just good old vanilla javascript, DOM manipulation, CSS, and HTML. I make things. It is what I have always done.

I started an escape room in 2015 where I plan, build all original puzzles (that are then strung together to create a cohesive story and line of things to do for out customers). Through creating 6 fully functioning, hour long escape room "adventures", I began to learn computer science and web development. This started as a way to make a few intresting puzzles for my business, but then it stuck. I love coding.. To further my learing I applied to Fullstack Academy of Code, completed the program and was immediatly hired to become a Teaching Fellow for the next class. I loved the face pace learning (learning a full stack (NERDs stack) in less than three month and going on to create fully functioning apps. It was difficult, but so rewarding to leave with that wealth of knowledge I am now applying). Currently i am seeking full-time work as a Software Enineer, so please take a look at this site, my resume, and let me know how we can work together to build the next cool thing. Thank you.
